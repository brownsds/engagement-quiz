How effective are you at influencing your business stakeholders?

A self-assessment quiz for leaders in audit, risk, GC, CoSec or compliance

Instructions: Choose the answer that best reflects your typical approach. Your score will be calculated automatically at the end.

1. When raising a sensitive issue with a senior business leader, what's your default approach?

2. A senior stakeholder dismisses your concerns in a meeting. What happens next?

3. How would your stakeholders describe your communication style?

4. When a key project is going off track, how do you intervene?

5. What role do you play in senior decision-making forums?

6. You're asked to compromise on something you think really matters. What do you do?

7. How do you secure commitment to meaningful action from business stakeholders?
